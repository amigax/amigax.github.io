Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Every feature is built with one obsession: speed. No polling loops. No background services. No nonsense.

🖥️

One Bar Per Monitor

A fully independent taskbar on every display. Each bar shows only the windows living on that monitor, keeping your workflow clean and contextual.

🖼️

Custom Textures Pro

Use any image — wood grain, brushed metal, neon grids, your own art — as the bar background. Stretch it or tile it. Looks like nothing else on Windows.

✨

Acrylic Blur Pro

Real Windows acrylic blur effect — the same compositor trick Microsoft uses in Settings and Start Menu. GPU-rendered, zero CPU, looks stunning.

👁️

Thumbnail Previews Pro

Hover any taskbar icon to see a live preview of that window. DWM-rendered, the same technology as the native Windows taskbar. Fast and native.

📌

Pinned App Launcher

Pin your favourite apps for one-click launch. Dragged straight from Explorer or browsed. Icons auto-extracted from executables.

🔊

Volume Control

Master volume slider and mute toggle built right into the tray. Talks directly to the Windows Core Audio API. No third-party audio drivers needed.

🕐

Clock on Every Monitor

Time on every bar, date on hover. Because glancing at your secondary monitor shouldn't mean losing eye contact with your work.

⊞

Start Button Everywhere

A Start button on every monitor for quick access. Per-monitor position: top, bottom, left, right. Your setup, your rules.

🎨

Per-Monitor Colour Pro

Colour-code your monitors. Pick from presets or use the full colour picker. Each bar remembers its own colour, edge, alignment and texture independently.

🔌

Monitor Hot-Plug

Plug in a monitor mid-session and BarZilla! detects it instantly, spinning up a new bar automatically. Unplug and it cleans up. Zero fuss.

⚡

Event-Driven Engine

No polling loops. BarZilla! hooks Windows event APIs (WinEvents, WM_DISPLAYCHANGE, Core Audio) so it only wakes up when something actually happens.

🪟

Click to Focus / Minimise

Click once to bring a window forward, click again to minimise. Instant focus via AttachThreadInput — bypasses Windows' focus-stealing restrictions perfectly.
